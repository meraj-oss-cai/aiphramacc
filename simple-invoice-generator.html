📊 What is Simple Invoice Generator?

A lightweight desktop application that lets you create, customize, and print professional invoices in minutes. Perfect for freelancers, small businesses, shops, and service providers. Download once, use forever.

Why You'll Love It

Click & Create

Open the app and start creating invoices immediately. No learning curve, just straightforward invoicing.

Flexible Item List

Add as many items as you need. Remove items instantly. Complete control over your invoice structure.

Instant Calculations

Watch totals update in real-time as you enter quantities, prices, and discounts.

Global Discounts

NEW in v2.0: Apply percentage discounts to entire invoices with automatic tax recalculation.

Professional PDFs

Generate beautiful, print-ready PDFs instantly. Share via email or save for records.

Direct Printing

Print invoices directly without opening PDF viewers. Send straight to your printer in one click.

Easy Date Selection

Click to pick invoice dates from a calendar. No manual date typing required.

Works Everywhere

Windows, macOS, Linux. Same functionality, same experience on every platform.

  • 1 GB RAM

Common Questions

Is Simple Invoice Generator free?

Yes! Download once and use forever. No subscriptions, no hidden costs, no licensing fees.

No. The app works completely offline. Create invoices, print, and save PDFs without any internet connection.

Is my invoice data secure?

Yes. All data stays on your computer. We never upload, store, or access your invoices.
