Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get a demo?

Fill out the contact form above selecting "Request Demo". Our team will reach out via email to schedule a personalized demo within 24 hours.

What's the pricing structure?

Free: All features + AI (no support) | $20: All features + AI (no support) | $40: All features + AI + email support. View our pricing page for details.

Do all plans include AI?

Yes! Free, $20, and $40 plans ALL include Claude AI and Google Gemini analytics. The difference is support—Free and $20 are self-service, $40 includes priority email support.

What's the difference between $20 and $40?

$20 Starter: All features + AI, self-service (no email support). $40 Professional: All features + AI + 24-hour email support, priority bug fixes, maintenance updates.

Do I pay extra for AI usage?

Yes, AI usage charges are separate for all plans. Pay separately for Claude API and Google Gemini based on actual consumption—transparent and fair.

When should I upgrade to $20 or $40?

$20: When you want to use AI features (Free doesn't include them). $40: When you need email support, priority bug fixes, and maintenance—for mission-critical operations.

Can I use Free forever?

Yes! No time limit. All features including AI, no support needed. Perfect for testing or non-critical use. No credit card required.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade/downgrade between Free, $20, or $40 instantly. Pay only for your current tier. No contracts, no penalties.

Do you offer integrations or API access?

Yes! We can discuss custom integrations and API access. Contact us at support@cloudaisoft.com with your specific requirements.

Is there data migration help available?

Yes, we can assist with migrating your existing pharmacy data. Customers on the With AI + Support plan get dedicated assistance with this process.
