Our Story

CloudAiSoft was founded in 2026 by a team of passionate technologists and entrepreneurs who believed that AI could transform how businesses operate. We started with a simple mission: to bring cutting-edge AI technology to enterprise software in a way that is accessible, reliable, and impactful.

We're a startup on a mission to revolutionize pharmacy management with our flagship product, AIPharmAccount. Built with Claude AI and Google Gemini, we're creating intelligent solutions that solve real business problems.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our vision, always keeping our early customers' success at the center of everything we do. We're excited to grow alongside the businesses that believe in our mission.
