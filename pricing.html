Frequently Asked Questions About Pricing

Is the Free plan really completely free?

Yes! All features included - inventory, billing, AI analytics, reports, unlimited users. No official support (self-service only). Forever free.

What's the difference between the three plans?

Free ($0): All features + AI, self-service only. Paid ($20): All features + AI + official support (but NOT for AI). Professional ($40): All features + AI + official support (including for AI).

Do all plans include AI features?

Yes! All three plans (Free, Paid, Professional) include Claude AI and Google Gemini analytics. The difference is support - Free: none, Paid: non-AI only, Professional: full including AI.

What support do I get for AI features?

Free & Paid: None. You use AI yourself (self-service). Professional ($40): Full support including help with AI features.

Do I pay extra for AI usage?

Yes! All plans include AI features. Claude API and Google Gemini usage charges are billed separately - pay only for what you use.

Which plan should I choose?

Free: Testing or non-critical use. $20: Small pharmacy, self-service comfortable. $40: Business-critical, need email support & priority fixes.

Can I upgrade/downgrade anytime?

Yes! Switch between plans instantly. Pay only for the current month at your chosen tier. No long-term contracts.

What if I find a bug?

Free & $20: You can report it, but no guarantees. If it's critical (affects everyone), we'll fix it. $40: Priority bug fixes - we resolve within 48 hours.

Why would I pay $40 instead of $20?

Professional support - someone to answer your questions, fix bugs fast, and maintain your system. For businesses relying on the software daily, this gives you peace of mind.

Is Free really forever?

Yes! No time limit, no expiration. Use it forever, including AI features. The only trade-off: no support or maintenance promises. You manage it yourself.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Contact us for volume discounts and annual billing options. We're flexible for customers with specific needs.
