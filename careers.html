QA Tester - Internship

Internship (Final Year)
3-6 Months
Remote

We're looking for a passionate final-year college student to join our team as a QA Tester Intern. This is a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience testing our AI-powered pharmacy management system (AIPharmAccount). You'll work with a team of experienced developers, learn quality assurance best practices, and contribute to a real enterprise product.

What You'll Do

  • Perform manual testing of web and desktop applications (JavaFX)
  • Identify and document software bugs with clear steps to reproduce
  • Create test cases and test scenarios for different features
  • Test the AI-powered invoice parser and pharmacy management features
  • Learn quality assurance methodologies and best practices
  • Collaborate with the development team and provide feedback
  • Help improve product quality and user experience

About You

  • Currently in final year of B.Tech/MCA/BCA or equivalent degree
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving mindset
  • Basic understanding of software development concepts
  • Familiarity with Java or any programming language (preferred)
  • Excellent communication and reporting skills
  • Ability to work independently and collaborate with team
  • Eager to learn and grow in QA/Testing domain
  • Enthusiasm for technology and software quality

A Plus If You Have

  • Basic knowledge of testing frameworks like TestNG or JUnit
  • Experience with Git/GitHub for version control
  • Familiarity with healthcare/pharmacy domain
  • Experience writing test cases or test documentation
  • Understanding of databases and SQL

Internship Details

  • Duration: 3-6 Months (Flexible)
  • Type: Full-time Internship
  • Location: Remote
  • Academic Credit: Can be counted towards final year project/internship requirement
  • Mentorship: You'll be guided by experienced QA and development team
  • Start Date: Immediate or as per your schedule
